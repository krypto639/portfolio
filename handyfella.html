Handy Fella

Approached by the client I was tasked with creating a digital and physical identity to promote a recently established general maintenance and painting company. The name Handy Fella was selected to represent the new brand because of its relation to the work as a way of expressing someones ability to perform a wide variety of jobs which the company wished to represent. Drawing inspiration from the client's Scottish background I then used blue as a core colour in the branding along with a character logo that symbolized the can do attitude of the brand. Final outputs of the project were business cards, personalized notepad and responsive website design, visitable at www.handyfella.co.nz
