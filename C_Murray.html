Cameron Murray Portfolio

Having known the client I knew that having recently entered into professional photography shoots for wild tomato that he was looking to have a separate digital platform from his social media to store his collection of professional, personal and travel photography. Working together with the client we gathered inspiration from visiting photographers websites like samuel elkins and samuel david smith to inform the layout presentation. Challenges in the design were the responsive coding and working with flex box layouts to create pages that would present a collection of portrait and landscape photography together smoothly.
