Crunch Time

Approached by 'Ernest Adams' cookies after a decline in their sales to their competitor 'Farmbake', we were asked to analyse existing macro and micro trends to reinvent the brand with a new range of flavors to steal purchases from Farmbake. Reviewing the current packaging of Ernest Adams we felt the image of home was outdated and did not speak to its current audience, with its flavor range offered very little difference from its competition selection. Reinventing the brand we decided to focus on urbanization, connecting the brand with urban audiences and young professionals who want something tasty while their busy on the go. To make our brand more appealing to our chosen audience we designed the packaging with a resealable design for reaching consumer on the go and selected the name ‘Crunch Time’ to represent the activity, deadlines and busy nature of the city. Drawing inspiration from the brands history we selected old flavors and gave them a modern twist. The brands new flavor range includes ‘white choclate and lemon; dark chocolate and rasperry; dark chocolate oats and honey; vanilla toffee, salted carmel and milk chocolate and shortbread and thyme’. Choosing to emphasize the flavor of the cookie rather than the setting of home we choose to use a collage of the cookie, its ingredients, colours and wooden texture background to reflect a urban connection of nature to the city.
