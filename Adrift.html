Adrift

  • Deliverables
  • app
  • Design
  • Kyle Whyte
Adrift explores how experience design can be used to simplify transitions between cultures for travelers. Users are able to share their experiences, connect with others and engage with their location. Adrift also offers guides for adapting to different situations, minimizing mental and psychological strains associated with living abroad.
