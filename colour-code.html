Colour Code is a colouring in stall created for Massey Universities creative industrys paper. The brief was to create a non profit stall providing an offering or activity for people to interact with in a shared marketplace. Our groups offering took inspiration from the recent sucess of colouring in books as a form of stress relief, we then used kerby rosanes work from sketchy stories as an inspiration for how we collectively contributed to the final outputs.
