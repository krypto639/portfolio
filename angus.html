Irn Bru Redesign

'Angus' is a concept redesign of the current mascot of 'Irn Bru'. The design recognizes the patriotic element of the drink being proudly owned by Scotland. It presents a cheeky, humorous character which would appeal to young and old, while maintaining characteristics true to it Scottish roots.
